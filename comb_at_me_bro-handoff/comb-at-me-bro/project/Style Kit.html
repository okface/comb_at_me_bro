Ink filters — 4 SVG <filter>S

Hand-drawn feel comes from displacement-mapped feTurbulence applied to clean SVG strokes. Four strengths cover everything: light wobble for type, default for sprites, strong for clouds, plus paper-grain overlay.

rough-1 · light

Subtle wobble. Type-friendly. Used for thin strokes & small sprites.

rough-2 · default

Balanced wobble. The workhorse for bee & structure outlines.

rough-3 · strong

Aggressive wobble. Smoke clouds, AoE shapes, blobs.

paper

paper-grain

High-frequency noise tinted with ink. Overlay for paper feel.

Animation primitives — 8 LOOPS

Compose-from-these. Sprites bob, hives breathe, damaged things shake, status icons pulse, dying enemies curl, numbers rise. Pure CSS keyframes; no JS state required.
