A · FLAT VECTOR

Best for: scale & speed

Hive choice
Man-made box — silhouette reads as "tower."
Strengths
Scales infinitely, fastest to author, strongest 32px read.
Risks
Can feel generic — needs strong color & motion to feel alive.
B · HAND-DRAWN

Best for: warmth & charm

Hive choice
Wild nest — leans into storybook tone.
Strengths
Distinctive, marketable, premium feel. Stand-out in store.
Risks
Heaviest art lift; ink wobble can muddy the smallest silhouettes.
C · PIXEL ART

Best for: nostalgia & clarity

Hive choice
Hanging comb — classic game language.
Strengths
Crispest small-scale silhouettes; great for animation later.
Risks
Needs disciplined palette & HUD type to avoid retro pastiche.
